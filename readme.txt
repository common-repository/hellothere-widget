== Description ==
HelloThere plugin actively engages your visitors by asking them relevant questions in order to convert them into leads and customers.
HelloThere makes it too simple.
Just compose your questions and define the rules that trigger them. HelloThere will take care of the rest. By doing so, your site visitors will want to engage with your site and become your customers.
The best part is that more than 73% of users provide their e-mail address after having the feeling of helping someone else and engage with hellothere widget.

Moreover, Hellothere helps you organize customer relationships like a boss. Our advanced tracking software collects detailed customer data and allows you to follow up with a personalized touch.

Features

* Fully customizable
* Manage your customers
* Analytics
* Contact Forms
* Widget behaviour management
* User activity and note tracking

== Installation ==
1. Upload `hellothere-widget.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the `Plugins` menu in WordPress
3. Log into your `HelloThere` account and configure your widget
4. Press the `Get code` button and copy your widget's code from the `Wordpress` tab
5. Go to the `HelloThere` admin area of your WordPress
6. Paste your code into the `Widget key` field and press `Save changes`

Alternatively:

1. Login to your WordPress admin area
2. Search for `HelloThere`
3. Click install `HelloThere Widget`
4. Activate the plugin through the `Plugins` menu in WordPress
5. Log into your `HelloThere` account and configure your widget
6. Press the `Get code` button and copy your widget's code from the `Wordpress` tab
7. Go to the `HelloThere` admin area of your WordPress
8. Paste your code into the `Widget key` field and press `Save changes`
